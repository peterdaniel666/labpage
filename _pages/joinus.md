<h4><strong>Opportunies</strong></h4>
<br>

<h5><strong>Postdoctoral Fellows</strong></h5>
<p align = "justify">
We welcome motivated postdoctoral fellows to complement our multi-disciplinary team. Candidates with a background in synthetic biology, drug development, metabolomics, or biomedical engineering are warmly encouraged. Please email Yihan your CV, representative publications from the past five years, and a brief statement outlining your proposed research plan. Please also include the names and contact details of two references, including your graduate advisor.
</p>
<br>

<h5><strong>Graduate Students</strong></h5>
<p align = "justify">
We’re looking for enthusiastic graduate students interested in synthetic biology, drug development, or biomedical engineering. Please email Yihan your CV, previous academic transcripts, and a brief statement describing your research interests and reasons for applying.
</p>
<br>

<h5><strong>Undergraduates</strong></h5>
<p align = "justify">
We encourage ambitious undergraduates seeking research experience to join us. Please email Yihan your CV, previous academic transcripts, and a brief statement describing your research interests, reasons for applying, and how you would benefit from working with our team.
</p>
<br>

<h5><strong>Research Assistants (wet lab technician)</strong></h5>
<p align = "justify">
We invite applications for research assistant roles to support our wet lab work. Please email Yihan your CV, previous academic transcripts, and a brief statement describing your interests and why you’d like to join our lab.
</p>
